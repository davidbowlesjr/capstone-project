Elastic Compute Cloud

Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2) is a fundamental component of Amazon Web Services. Amazon EC2 offers a flexible and scalable solution for businesses and developers, providing the infrastructure needed to deploy applications, host websites, process data, and perform various computational tasks.

With Amazon EC2, you have the freedom to choose from a diverse selection of virtual machine instances, catering to different computing requirements, such as CPU, memory, storage, and network performance. Whether you need on-demand resources for quick, short-term projects or you want to optimize costs with reserved instances for long-term workloads, Amazon EC2 offers a variety of pricing options to suit your needs.
