Simple Notification Service

Amazon Simple Notification Service (SNS) is a fully managed messaging service that enables users to send notifications and messages to a variety of recipients or endpoints.

With SNS, you can easily orchestrate and automate the dissemination of critical information, alerts, and updates to a diverse audience. Whether you need to notify users about system events, broadcast promotional offers, or deliver time-sensitive alerts, SNS simplifies the process and ensures that messages are delivered reliably.
